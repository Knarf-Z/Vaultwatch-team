Vaultwatch Research is a student-led research group working on security economics, decentralized finance risk, and public-data audits of protocol incentives.

Current focus

Do DeFi bug bounties match the money at risk?

Our first project studies whether public bug bounty ceilings scale with publicly observable value at risk. We use sources such as Immunefi and DeFiLlama to compare bounty rules, critical reward caps, scope restrictions, and protocol size.
